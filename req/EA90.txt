Cadastral service request

The citizen accesses the municipal land registry service area through the appropriate link
The system displays the form for entering the data necessary for the request
The citizen inserts the requested data
The system verifies the correctness and completeness of the data then shows a summary screen or activates the accordance of the incorrect data
The citizen confirms the operation
The system displays the payment methods
The citizen makes the choice
The system sends a notification message of the request to the competent employee
The system notifies the citizen that the request was forwarded and the copy is soon sent
