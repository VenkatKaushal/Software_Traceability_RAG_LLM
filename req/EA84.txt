Present building project

The citizen accesses the building project presentation area through the appropriate link
The system displays the form for the insertion of the necessary data
The citizen inserts the requested data
The system verifies the correctness and completeness of the data then shows a summary screen or activates the accordance of the incorrect data
The citizen confirms the operation
The system displays the print form
The system sends a notification message of the request to the competent employee
The citizen carries out the press to be delivered to the municipal office with attachments the requested documents and a stamp duty
