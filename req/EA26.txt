Change of residence Other municipality

The citizen presses the change of residence on the button
The system shows the citizen the form to be completed
The citizen inserts the data of his new home
The system notifies the citizen the correct sending of the request and this is memorized
The employee accesses the system and displays the received request
The employee checks the data fills out the document with the rest of the data to be inserted and confirmed the operation
The system saves the form and update the data
The employee sends a document with the request made by the citizen to the employee of the other municipality
The other commune employee checks the document and sends a positive notification to the other employee and a notification to the citizen
