Minor Mothers Assistance

The user selects the social assistance function by selecting assistance request to mothers and minors in difficulty
The system shows the form for the insertion of the applicant's data and the recipient
The user enters his own data and those of the recipient of the request himself another person if necessary specifying the relationship with that person
Also specifies a possible date of appointment with social workers
Then submit the form
The system picks up the personal data of the citizen recipient and the data concerning the family unit of the citizen recipient
The request is sent to the Social Services Office and the sending is notified to the user
